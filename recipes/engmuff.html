English muffins

Get dem buns warm and toasty

Now that I drove everyone away with that title, we can focus on some buns

I adapted this for euros because well...corn is a USA thing

Ingredients

  • 450g flour
  • packet of yeast(17g)
  • 125 ml whole milk
  • 125 ml of warm water
  • Tbsp of sugar
  • Tsp of salt
  • Butter

Putting it all together

  1. Combine millk, water and yeast, mix it up and leave it for 15 mins
  2. Mix flour, sugar and salt
  3. Combine the liquids and the solids and knead 'em
  4. I SAID KNEAD 'EM
  5. When it goes from hetero to homo, you let that thing rest for an hour
  6. After the hour is up, you take that big ball of dough, deflate it and roll it thicc
  7. Not too thick, like the thickness of your thumb thick, if you have the mits of a sailor, use a ruler or your appendage
  8. After rolling it out, take a cup, cut them circles out, lightly cover them with some bread crumbs if you have any, or don't, not that big of a deal
  9. Put the things on a skillet, and on a medium temp toast them. At the same time, heat up the oven to 90Â°C and after toasting just toss them in
  10. After the last one is done toasting, leave them in for another 10 minutes and then turn off the heat and put them on a rack to cool down

Now put it all on a tray and serve with some jam, bakalar, whatever, it's bread for christsake's
